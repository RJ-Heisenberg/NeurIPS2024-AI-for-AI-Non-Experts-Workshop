AI for AI Non-Experts Workshop (application in progress)

NeurIPS 2024 Affinity Event

Workshop Details

Group Name: AI for AI Non-Experts

Contact Person: Rujun Gao, Texas A&M University, grj1214@tamu.edu, +1 (979)-321-3135

Purpose and Goals: We propose a workshop to share and exchange ideas about building AI for AI non-experts and to discuss the following questions:

  • How to educate AI non-experts in the era of GenAI? How may AI help instructors?
  • How to help AI non-experts? What may be proper topics, materials, and methods?
  • How and what to educate AI non-experts about AIâ€™s ethical and societal issues?
  • How do AI non-experts modify, adopt and customize AI in their workflows?
  • How do we design AI for non-experts?
  • How does AI expertise play into risk assessment and ethical use of AI?
  • Does one need to be an expert in AI to assess its risks?
